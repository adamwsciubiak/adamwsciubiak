About Me

I am a psychologist with expertise in UX design, cognitive science, and artificial intelligence. My current research focuses on developing AI-driven cognitive-behavioral interventions for mental health. I hold a Master's degree in Psychology from the University of Wrocław and a postgraduate degree in UX & Product Design from SWPS University. My goal is to apply AI to create scalable, personalized tools for emotion regulation in educational and therapeutic settings.

Projects

My ongoing research involves the development of an Affective Intelligent Agent Model for enhancing emotion regulation in adult learners. This AI-driven model adapts to users' emotional states to provide personalized cognitive-behavioral interventions.

Past research includes my Master's thesis, "Mindfulness in Yoga Practitioners and Meditators and Affective-Stimulated Verbal Creativity," where I explored the relationship between mindfulness and creativity using mixed methods.

I also worked on a wearable-based project to measure cognitive effort using EEG and heart rate data.
