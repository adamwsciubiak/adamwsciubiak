About Me

I am a psychologist with expertise in UX design, cognitive science, and artificial intelligence. My current research focuses on developing AI-driven cognitive-behavioral interventions for mental health. I hold a Master's degree in Psychology from the University of Wrocław and a postgraduate degree in UX & Product Design from SWPS University. My goal is to apply AI to create scalable, personalized tools for emotion regulation in educational and therapeutic settings.

Research Projects

Current Projects

My ongoing research involves the development of an Affective Intelligent Agent Model for enhancing emotion regulation in adult learners. This AI-driven model adapts to users' emotional states to provide personalized cognitive-behavioral interventions. The project aims to make mental health interventions more accessible and scalable.

Past Research

Past research includes my Master's thesis, "Mindfulness in Yoga Practitioners and Meditators and Affective-Stimulated Verbal Creativity," where I explored the relationship between mindfulness and creativity using mixed methods. I also worked on a wearable-based project to measure cognitive effort using EEG and heart rate data.

Future Plans

In the future, I aim to expand the use of AI in mental health tools, focusing on scalability, accessibility, and personalized interventions for diverse user groups.

Professional Experience

Academic Roles

I have been actively involved in academic research, including being the originator of the Neuropsychology Student Club "Cerebro" at the University of Wrocław. I have participated in neuroscience forums and hackathons, such as Brainhack Krakow and Neuromatch Academy, working on projects like analyzing mouse social behavior using machine learning.

Industry Experience

As a psychologist, I have worked with children, adolescents, and adults in various settings, including kindergartens and nursing homes. In UX design, I led a team to develop a medical documentation management app during my postgraduate studies, which was recognized for its innovation. I also worked as an instructional designer, where I created training materials and coordinated UX/UI research projects.
